Mahabodhi Mission School (MMS) is English medium co-educational school for the boys and the girls under CBSE CURRICULUM with classes from nursery to Std XIIth, Run & Managed by â€œShekhar Educational Trust, Patna". The school believes in Lord Buddha's Marg of eliminating darkness of illiteracy by enlightening the lamp of education & knowledge.


The school is a dream project of Sri Dinesh Sharma son of Late Ramchandra Sharma a resident of Kako, Jehanabad. He recognized the need of the village and adjoining areas which is lagging far behind in terms of Quality Education. The school will try hard to bridge this gap by providing opportunities to motivate the students to register exemplary success in the field of education.


We promise to dedicate our efforts and work relentlessly to bring laurels to the school in every sphere, beside academic all efforts will be made to have the blending of academics and extra curricular activities for holistic personality development of every student. The school will take care of the mental, physical, creative and imaginative development of its students without putting undue strain on them


MISSION


  • To identify the flower in the bud, to recognize the potential of each child,
  • To appreciate with passion and sensitivity.
  • To convert potential into performance.

We embark on the mission of creating complete individuals who are confident about their potential diligent to work towards their goal, sensitive to their environment. Thus, we aim at development of self-confidence among students.


Our Aim is to facilitate a child's self-exploration journey as he assumes the role of an individual, a social being and a global citizen, above all, we ensure that our children grow-up with due patriotism.


VISION


OUR ENDEAVOR- To develop in our children a love for learning. To develop well rounded personalities who would be confident, creative and be able to adjust and adapt to any circumstances of environment.


To create an environment that would be conducive to learning, a place where children are happy to be part of it, a place they love and cherish.


The sole vision for the establishment of this institution is to provide right to education to every child irrespective of caste, creed and social status .
